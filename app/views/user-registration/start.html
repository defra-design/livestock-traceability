Livestock Information Service (LIS) for cattle

Use this service to:

  • Report births, deaths and movements of your cattle
  • Maintain an online holding register
  • View an animal's digital record
  • Pre-notify other keepers of movements
  • Edit animal details (where allowed)
  • Track herd performance with the Keeper Insights dashboard

Create an account

To create an account, you'll need:

  • Your County Parish Holding (CPH) number
  • Your email address associated with the CPH number
  • To be registered as a cattle keeper with the Rural Payments Agency (RPA) and the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA)

About the Livestock Information Service

The Livestock Information Service (LIS) is th eofficial cattle traceability service for England – replacing the Cattle Tracing system previously managed by the British Cattle Movement Service (BCMS).

LIS provides a secure, single sign-on platform for livestock keepers and those across the supply chain to report cattle movements in England.
