Livestock Information Service (LIS) for cattle

Report cattle births, movements and deaths, browse your animal’s digital record and manage your holding details.

Use this service to:

  • Report births, deaths and movements of your cattle
  • Maintain an online record of animals on your holding
  • View an animal's digital record
  • Edit animal details (where allowed)
  • View and update your holding details

Sign in or create an account

You’ll need to sign into your Defra account to use the Livestock Information Service. If you do not have an account, you will be able to create one.

To create an account, you'll need:

  • your County Parish Holding (CPH) number
  • your email address associated with the CPH number
  • to be registered as a cattle keeper with the Rural Payments Agency (RPA) and the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA)

About the Livestock Information Service

The Livestock Information Service (LIS) is the official cattle traceability service for England – replacing the Cattle Tracing System (CTS) managed by the British Cattle Movement Service (BCMS).

LIS provides a secure, single sign-on platform for livestock keepers and those across the supply chain to report cattle births, movements and deaths in England.
