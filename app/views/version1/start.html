Before you start

If you're using the service for the first time, you have the choice to create an account.

You'll need to already be registered with the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) and have a flock or herd mark..

You'll need:

  • your county parish holding (CPH) number
  • the email address you used to register your CPH number
