If you don't have an account already

Follow the 'Sign in with GOV.UK One Login' option to create an account.

What to expect

Creating the account can take up to 15 minutes if you don't already have a GOV.UK One Login account. You will be sent a confirmation code to your mobile phone.

After creating a GOV.UK One Login account, we will ask for additional information.

Accessing this service for work or on behalf of an organisation

If you are accessing this service on behalf of a business or organisation for work purposes, set up a new GOV.UK One Login account with your work email address.

We will then ask for additional information about your business or organisation.

You will be able to invite other users to join your organisation after you created your Defra account.
