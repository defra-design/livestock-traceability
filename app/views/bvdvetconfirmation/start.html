Confirm the official bovine viral diarrhoea (BVD) status of a cattle herd

If you are a vet, you must use this service to tell Defra the official BVD status of a cattle herd in England.

You will be asked to:

  • review recent BVD laboratory test results for a herd (including tag and test, check testing, or bulk milk samples)
  • decide and allocate the final BVD herd status: negative, working towards negative, not negative, positive

Before you start

You must be a registered veterinary surgeon to use this service. Have your RCVS login details ready. Defra wil use your RCVS number to verify your identity and ensure only authorised professionals can update official animal health records.
