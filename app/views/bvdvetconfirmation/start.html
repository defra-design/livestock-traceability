BVD Herd Status Confirmation (England)

Vets will use this service as part of the mandatory BVD eradication programme to confirm the official BVD status of a cattle herd.

Use this service to:

  • Review recent BVD laboratory test results (including tag and test, check testing, or bulk milk samples) for a herd
  • Decide and allocate the final BVD herd status:
    • Negative
    • Working towards negative
    • Not negative
    • Positive
